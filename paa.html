Alan Mathison Turing

Born on June 23, 1912 at Paddington, London. Turing is the the youngest of the two children of Julius Mathison and Ethel Sara Turing. His family belonged to the upper-middle-class, with his father working for the Indian Civil Service (ICS) and his mother as the daughter of the engineer of the Madras Railway.
